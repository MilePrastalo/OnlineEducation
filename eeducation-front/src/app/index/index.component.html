Eeducation

Free online education website

This platform is free to use and create

Create courses

You have the ability to create and manage courses. Whether you are teacher or school, you can create better courses that ever before

Manage students

Chose who gets to study your courses. You have full control who gets to join your school. Students can send you request to join your school or you can invite them

Grade students

You have the ability to add grades to your students using test or manually

Manage absences

Did student missed day in school or zoom meeting? You can use our system to keep track of absent students

Tests

You can create tests for your students and choose do you want to grade them yourself or have automatic grading
